Smart Tank (Hydro Protect IOT)

Currently, Nuevo León is facing a water crisis that has prompted the state government to implement alternative measures, such as scheduling water supply cuts at different times depending on the area. In response to this situation, we have developed an Internet of Things (IoT) project focused on a smart water tank.

This tank has been designed and programmed to perform various functions, including:

  • Real-time monitoring of the available water level in the tank, expressed in liters.
  • Recording measurements that include time, water quantity, pH level, and storage capacity.
  • Connecting the project to a database through an API.
  • Incorporating a pH sensor to maintain continuous control of the liquid's conditions, allowing us to detect any deviations from normal ranges. In the event of such an occurrence, an actuator would trigger the dispensing of a tablet.
  • And more functions.

This collaboratively developed project addressed the social issue of water scarcity comprehensively, without neglecting the safety and integrity of individuals. For more detailed information, a link to the repository and generated documents will be provided.
